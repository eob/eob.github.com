Ted Benson

I am a Ph.D. student at MIT CSAIL advised by David Karger. My research is driven by a desire to see better use of structured data on the web. This desire has materialized in work on web architecture, natural language processing, and data publication tools.

Prior to coming to MIT, I did research and development for three years at BBN Technologies. I got my B.S. in Computer Science at the University of Virginia.
