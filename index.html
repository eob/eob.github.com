Ted Benson

Hello.

I am a Ph.D. student at MIT CSAIL advised by David Karger. My research is driven by a desire to see better use of structured data on the web. I'm currently working on Cascading Tree Sheets, which does for web structure what CSS did for style. I've also done a bit of natural language processing work, which you're using whenever you talk to your Android phone. Prior to coming to MIT, I did research and development for three years at BBN Technologies.

Last semester I created the curriculum for and taught 6.S194 - Open Source Software Project Lab. I moonlight as a hacker in residence at Klaviyo.
