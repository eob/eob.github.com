Ted Benson

Hello.

I am a Ph.D. student at MIT CSAIL advised by David Karger. My research is driven by a desire to see better use of structured data on the web. Most of my work is on web architecture, natural language processing, and data publication tools. I'm currently working on Cascading Tree Sheets. Prior to coming to MIT, I did research and development for three years at BBN Technologies. I earned my B.S. in Computer Science at the University of Virginia.

Last semester I created the curriculum for and taught 6.S194 - Open Source Software Project Lab. I moonlight as a hacker in residence at Klaviyo.
